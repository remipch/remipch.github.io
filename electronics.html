Relays test bench

At Idtsoft I built special boards to test a complete set of relays for the SNCF.

The principle was to generate some specific electrical signals, apply them to the correct relay contact and measure the response:

  • commutation times
  • switching currents
  • contact conductivities
  • dielectric strength
  • response to particular electrical configurations
